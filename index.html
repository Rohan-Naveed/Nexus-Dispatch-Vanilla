Dry Van

Is an enclosed truck trailer with a solid roof and sides. It's used for transporting general cargo that doesn't require temperature control.

Reefer

Is designed to transport temperature-sensitive cargo. It has a refrigeration unit connected to the power supply onboard.

Flat Bed/Step Deck

Has an open, flat platform without sides or a roof. It's ideal for carrying oversized or irregularly shaped cargo.

Power Only

Refers to a service where third-party carriers provide the truck while another company supplies the trailers.

Box Truck

Have a fully enclosed cargo area. They're versatile and commonly used for local deliveries or moving services.

Hot Shot

Involves hauling time-sensitive or high-priority cargo, often with smaller trucks or vans for urgent deliveries.
